"Mountain Child" (2017)

"Mountain Child" is a short story about a tiny mountain spirit who wants to become a human boy. It's quite simple but I enjoyed the planning and drawing process. This is one of the first comics I've ever finished, so the narrative still holds a special place in my heart.

"Hello Apollo" (2018)

"Hello Apollo" is a short vignette that looks into the thoughts of a lonely astronaut who wishes she could go back home despite knowing what she wants is impossible. I wanted to explore a style of comic art where I created a narrative using whole pages as compositions instead of relying on fractured panels and speech bubbles to tell my story.
