Cindy Xia is a Chinese-American illustrator currently pursuing a BFA in Illustration at the Laguna College of Art and Design. Their works are focused on storytelling and environments, typically in the form of digital and traditional paintings. They enjoy creating artwork about nature and loves exploring atmosphere, patterns, and experimental colors in their pieces.
