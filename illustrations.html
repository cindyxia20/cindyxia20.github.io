Illustrations for Homecooked (2020)

Homecooked is a turn-based narrative RPG, all about cooking! Check out the game development page here! In the illustrations done below, I was asked to draw using a crayon and pastel style inspired by children's books and the artwork of Louie Zong. I had free reign to draw any subjects I wanted, so I focused on environments and character designs.

Prop Art

These are some of the food props I created for Homecooked. The director asked me to draw in a simpler, playful style that still manages to look interesting, appetizing and fun. Look forward to seeing more food art in future demos!
